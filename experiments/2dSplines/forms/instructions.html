The shape matching game!

In this experiment you will play a fun communication game with another turker! In each round both of you are going to be given a set of loopy shapes which will look like this:

You'll each see the same 3 shapes but the order will be different.

One of you will be assigned the Speaker role and the other will be the Listener . For each round, one of the three objects is the target, which can be identified by a black box around it. Only the speaker can see which object is the target. The task of the speaker is to tell the listener which one of the objects is the target and the listener in turn needs to select the right object based on this information provided by the speaker. Remember that it doesn't make sense to describe the location of the target object, since the order of the images is different for the listener and the speaker.

Only the speaker can see which one is the target.

The task of the listener to identify the correct color based on what the speaker has said. In order to communicate, you're given a chatbox where you can send messages back and forth to each other. The speaker can say whatever they need to indicate which shape is the target, and the listener is free to ask for clarifications if what the speaker said is not clear. Some rounds will be easier and some will be harder.

Once the speaker sends a message and the listener clicks on the object they believe is the target, both players will be given feedback (the speaker will see what the listener clicked, and the listener will see the true target), and you will both be automatically forwarded to the next round of objects. You will both get a 1 cent bonus for each round where the listener correctly identifies the target, for a total of 50 rounds. After the final round you will fill out a quick 15 second survey and be on your way.

A few final notes: First, since you are playing with another turker, you may see this screen before the game begins:

Just hold tight. Another turker should join the game within the next few minutes. If you're in the waiting room 15 minutes without another player joining, your HIT will automatically be submitted and accepted, out of gratitude for the time you spent waiting.

Second, please be respectful of the turker you're playing with: they're real people, so do not send rude or inappropriate material through the chat box (including links or screenshots), and try to be as fast and responsive as possible to respect your partner's time: it can be very frustrating if your partner suddenly disappears and makes the task longer than anticipated. If your partner disconnects in the middle of the game, you will be sent to the survey and allowed to submit the HIT for full compensation.

Finally, please DO NOT refresh the page at any point once you start the game (including the waiting page). If you refresh, you will not be able to rejoin the game you were in and you may not be able to submit the HIT.
Thank you!
